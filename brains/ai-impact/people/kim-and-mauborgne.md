# Who

W. Chan Kim and Renée Mauborgne, professors at INSEAD and co-directors of the INSEAD Blue Ocean Strategy Institute; co-authors of *Blue Ocean Strategy* (2005), one of the most influential strategy books of its era. They publish as an inseparable pair, so they share a note.

# Key idea

Value innovation: stop competing for share in bloody red oceans and create uncontested market space where the competition is irrelevant. This site revisits their playbook for the generative-AI era — see [Blue Ocean Strategy](../concepts/blue-ocean-strategy.md).
