# Who

Strategy adviser, author and former dean of the Rotman School of Management, repeatedly ranked among the most influential management thinkers. Co-author, with A.G. Lafley (former P&G CEO), of *Playing to Win: How Strategy Really Works* (2013).

# Key idea

Strategy is an integrated set of choices, not a plan — and its heart is two linked questions, *where to play* and *how to win*. See [Playing to Win](../concepts/playing-to-win.md).
