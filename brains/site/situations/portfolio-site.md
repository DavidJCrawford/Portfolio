# Summary

This Astro + MDX site (the repo this brain lives in). Implementation lives in the project's `spec.md` and `DESIGN.md`; this bundle holds the *thinking* behind it. The intellectual centre is the [through-line](../concepts/the-through-line.md): a place for [David](../people/david-j-crawford.md) to write, experimentally, about society and technology — and how [transdisciplinary design](../concepts/transdisciplinary-design.md) can help craft [product](../concepts/product.md) amid the [Nth-order effects](../concepts/nth-order-effects.md) of generative and agentic AI.

# Current state

Live as a curated exhibition of writing and projects, positioned around the craft of product to keep people [safe](../concepts/safe.md) and help them [thrive](../concepts/helping-people-thrive.md). This brain is published as a public [Open Knowledge Format](https://github.com/GoogleCloudPlatform/knowledge-catalog/tree/main/okf) bundle so people (and agents) can understand the project's context — an instance of [knowledge as leverage](../concepts/knowledge-as-leverage.md).
