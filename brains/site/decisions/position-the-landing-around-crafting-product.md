# Summary

The landing page is positioned around the craft of [product](../concepts/product.md) — the intended positioning from the outset. (The very first draft briefly opened from a generic “AI products succeed or fail at the intersection of model capability, human experience, and engineering reality” scaffold; that was a placeholder starting point, not the intent.)

# Decision

Lead with the craft of [product](../concepts/product.md) (the [3 lenses](../concepts/the-3-lenses-of-innovation.md)); name the *how* ([transdisciplinary design](../concepts/transdisciplinary-design.md) + [generative](../concepts/generative-ai.md) / [agentic AI](../concepts/agentic-ai.md)) and the *why* (keep people [safe](../concepts/safe.md) and [secure](../concepts/secure.md), help them [thrive](../concepts/helping-people-thrive.md), amid [change](../concepts/beset-with-change.md)). Tone: understated confidence.

# Rationale

Crafting product was always the destination. Model capability is real but not *interesting* to lead with, and the tone should be quietly confident rather than self-selling — idea-first, reflecting what the site is actually for.
