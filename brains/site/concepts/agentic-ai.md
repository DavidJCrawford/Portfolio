# Summary

AI that plans and takes actions toward goals — agents that orchestrate tools and workflows — extending [generative AI](./generative-ai.md) from producing to *doing*. It needs [guardrails and golden paths](./guardrails-and-golden-paths.md): the agent must respect the coherence of the system it works in, not explore freely. In the [engineering-is-not-construction](./engineering-is-not-construction.md) frame, agents are the construction company, which makes [knowledge and specifications](./knowledge-as-leverage.md) the leverage. Where stakes are high, [the agent proposes and a deterministic engine disposes](./deterministic-vs-probabilistic.md). And as agents shift from tools we drive to [delegates acting on our behalf](./agents-acting-for-humans.md), their [Nth-order effects](./nth-order-effects.md) — on trust, skills and institutions — become the real story.

# Core idea

From generating to doing. Agents change what a small team can feasibly build and run — and move human value to [wisdom work](./wisdom-work.md).
