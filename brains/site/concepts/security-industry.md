# Summary

The grounded professional context — physical security and access control, where AI product work meets real-world stakes. It is the concrete arena for the broader ideas on this site (the thinking generalises well beyond it), and a vivid case for [deterministic-vs-probabilistic](./deterministic-vs-probabilistic.md) design: a door cannot be “94% confident”.

# Why the arena is distinctive

The stakes are physical and the tolerances are tight: decisions actuate doors, gates and alarms in the real world; deployments are certified, regulated and audited; and the installed estate lives for decades, not sprint cycles. That's why the industry is such a vivid proving ground for AI product thinking — and why, as AI collapses softer advantages, its tight weld of digital control and physical reality is exactly the kind of [moat that holds](../../ai-impact/concepts/moats-after-ai.md).

# Core idea

The arena I work in — security & access control — but the ideas reach much further.
