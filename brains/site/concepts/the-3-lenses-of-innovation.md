# Summary

The core lenses of [Design Thinking](./design-thinking.md), popularised by IDEO and [Tim Brown](../people/tim-brown.md): good innovation lives in the overlap of [desirability](./desirability.md) (do people want it?), [feasibility](./feasibility.md) (can it be built and operated?), and [viability](./viability.md) (should it exist as a sustainable venture?). Strong [product](./product.md) sits in the overlap of all three; weak product over-indexes on one and ignores the others.

# Core idea

Product lives in the overlap of desirability, feasibility and viability. My preferred way to think about the work.
