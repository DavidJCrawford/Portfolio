# Summary

Not a thing but a craft — the deliberate shaping of solutions at the intersection of [desirability](./desirability.md), [feasibility](./feasibility.md) and [viability](./viability.md). The conviction is that product *can be crafted*, with intent and skill, rather than merely assembled. Practised through [transdisciplinary design](./transdisciplinary-design.md) and, increasingly, the leverage of [generative AI](./generative-ai.md) and [agentic AI](./agentic-ai.md).

# What the craft looks like with AI

When generation is cheap, the craft shifts from production to judgement: [curation](./curation.md) of strong options, clear [strategy](./good-strategy.md) (diagnosis before solution), and rigorous specification — because [the spec is the engineering](./engineering-is-not-construction.md), and [knowledge is the leverage](./knowledge-as-leverage.md).

# Core idea

Product is a craft, not a thing. It can be crafted — deliberately, well.
