# Summary

The condition of the present: relentless, compounding change — accelerated sharply by [generative AI](./generative-ai.md) and [agentic AI](./agentic-ai.md). The backdrop the work responds to; crafting product well is how we help people stay [safe](./safe.md) and [thrive](./helping-people-thrive.md) through it — the closing clause of [the through-line](./the-through-line.md).

# Leading through it

It calls for a posture more than a plan: shift from “know-it-all” to “learn-it-all”; distinguish *complicated* problems (a knowable answer with enough work) from *complex* ones (emergent, needing experimentation); and zoom out to the longer horizon, the wider stakeholder set, and the [Nth-order effects](./nth-order-effects.md) rather than optimising only what's in front of you.

# Core idea

The world is beset with change. The work is helping people stay safe and thrive through it.
