# Summary

The technical lens of the [3 Lenses of Innovation](./the-3-lenses-of-innovation.md): can it actually be built, operated and trusted? Engineering reality — the lens learned as a software engineer. [Generative AI](./generative-ai.md) and [agentic AI](./agentic-ai.md) are rapidly redrawing what is feasible, which is exactly why [the specification becomes the engineering](./engineering-is-not-construction.md) and why [AI engineering](./ai-engineering.md) is its own discipline. One corner of a three-way tension: what can be built matters only if people [want it](./desirability.md) and it can [sustain itself](./viability.md).

# Core idea

Can it be built, and trusted? The lens learned as a software engineer.
