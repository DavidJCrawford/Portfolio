# Summary

Beyond keeping people [safe](./safe.md): enabling them to flourish, not merely survive. The positive aim of the work — the difference between protection and genuine benefit. It connects to [desirability](./desirability.md) (solutions people actually want and are better for) and to [wisdom work](./wisdom-work.md) (freeing people for the judgement-rich work that matters).

# Thriving as agency

Thriving means *expanded agency*: technology that grows what people can do, understand and decide — not technology that quietly decides for them. That's the test to hold [agents acting on our behalf](./agents-acting-for-humans.md) against: delegation should hand people back time and reach for the judgement-rich work that matters, not hollow their judgement out. The [promoted doorman](../../ai-in-security/concepts/the-promoted-doorman.md) is the pattern done right.

# Core idea

Safe is the floor; thriving is the point.
