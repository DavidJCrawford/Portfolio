# Summary

AI that generates content, code, designs and more, collapsing the cost and time of creation. It is [probabilistic and creative](./deterministic-vs-probabilistic.md) — a primary driver of the world being [beset with change](./beset-with-change.md), a source of leverage in crafting [product](./product.md), and a force that keeps expanding [feasibility](./feasibility.md). When making becomes nearly free, [curation](./curation.md) becomes the bottleneck. Capability is no longer the interesting question; what we craft with it is. Strategically, it also collapses the scarcity premium that funded competitive moats — the [ai-impact brain](../../ai-impact/concepts/scarcity-collapse.md) follows that thread.

# Core idea

A new leverage for crafting product — interesting for what we make with it, not for raw capability.
