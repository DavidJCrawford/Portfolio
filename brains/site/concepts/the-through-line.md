# Summary

The single idea the site ladders up to. [Product](./product.md) is a craft. The interesting question is not how capable today's models are — they are remarkably capable — but how product gets *crafted*: at the intersection of [desirability](./desirability.md), [feasibility](./feasibility.md) and [viability](./viability.md) (the [3 Lenses of Innovation](./the-3-lenses-of-innovation.md)), through the lens, tools and techniques of [transdisciplinary design](./transdisciplinary-design.md), with the leverage of [generative AI](./generative-ai.md) and [agentic AI](./agentic-ai.md) — to build solutions that keep people [safe](./safe.md) and [secure](./secure.md) and help them [thrive](./helping-people-thrive.md), in a world [beset with change](./beset-with-change.md).

# The deeper concern

Underneath the craft is a systems question. Generative and agentic AI don't just change first-order tasks; their [Nth-order effects](./nth-order-effects.md) ripple through work, trust, skills and institutions. Reasoning about those effects needs [systems thinking](./systems-thinking.md) and the cross-disciplinary view of [transdisciplinary design](./transdisciplinary-design.md) — and it needs the human's [wisdom work](./wisdom-work.md) kept firmly at the centre while agents handle execution.

# Core idea

Less about me, more about the craft. A place to write about crafting product to keep people safe and help them thrive amid change — confident, never salesy.
