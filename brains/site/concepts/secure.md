# Summary

Security — protecting people, places and information from threats and unauthorised access or use. The professional domain (the [security industry](./security-industry.md)); the disciplined sibling of [safe](./safe.md). Its core promise is deterministic — the right people in at the right time, the wrong people out, every time — which is why AI here must [propose rather than dispose](./deterministic-vs-probabilistic.md).

# Means, not end

Security is the means; [safety](./safe.md) and [thriving](./helping-people-thrive.md) are the ends. Control of who, where and when is never the point in itself — it's what makes a hospital ward calm, a campus open, an airport fast. The craft is delivering the control without the fortress feeling: the strongest security is the kind the right people never notice.

# Core idea

Keep the wrong things out so the right things can happen.
